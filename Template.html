M.S. Dhoni

Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as M.S. Dhoni, is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, Dhoni is one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket.

Under his captaincy, India won the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007, the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011, and the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013. Known for his calm demeanor and exceptional leadership skills, Dhoni is also a wicketkeeper-batsman renowned for his finishing ability.
